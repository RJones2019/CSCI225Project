Other Information

These videos were shot using Xbox One's share feature. This allows you to capture up to five minutes of gameplay at one time and upload to Microsoft OneDrive. From there, Ghost uses Final Cut Pro to piece together the entertainment that is shown below. Final Cut Pro is an application for Macintosh users. If you're on Windows, unfortunately you won't have access to this powerful editing tool, but there are other options Adobe Premiere Pro works well for Windows as well as Mac users. Sony Vegas isn't recommended for beginners who aren't used to the layout of video editors For more information, the links to the developers websites are listed below.
