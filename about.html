About Our Clan!

Aviator Gaming is a regionally renowned professional gaming organization established in 2016 at USCA. Since our grassroots beginnings as a Call Of Duty clan and League of Legends community site, we have grown into a multisponsored esports Team with unparalleled reach in the industry. With over 4 titles and 6 world records under our belt, Aviator Gaming is synonymous with success. Our collective teams have won the most prize money in the Peach Belt Conference while amassing trophies across different titles and different consoles. Our main games in rotation for our team members are Call of Duty Vanguard, Halo Infinite, Rocket League, and League of Legends. More than just an esports team, Aviator Gaming is also a media powerhouse. Starting with our community sites, we have expanded into other areas within the gaming ecosystem, such as youtube and twitch. Today, Aviator Gaming continues to lead in both competition and innovation from our headquarters, the USCA Esports center, a state-of-the-art esports facility right on campus. With plans for further investments into different games and gaming ambitions, Aviator Gaming stands at the forefront of a thriving new industry.

PEACH BELT CONFERENCE

The Peach Belt Conference (PBC) is a collegiate athletic conference affiliated with the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA)'s Division II level. The ten member institutions are located in the South Atlantic states of South Carolina, Georgia, and Florida. In addition, five affiliate members participate in one sport each; namely sports not sponsored by their home conferences.
Since its inception in 1990-91, the Peach Belt has, across all sanctioned sports, produced 30 national champions and additional 27 national finalists. Starting with only two championships in 1991, in men's and women's basketball, the conference has expanded to 16 championship sports with the addition of women's golf in the fall of 2009 and men's lacrosse in the summer of 2020.
